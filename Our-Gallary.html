Jain Temple

To reach the temple, one need to make its way through the brilliantly decorated the entrance. The main hall of the temple adorns the statue of the Lord Neminath, the 22nd Jain Tirthankar. The temple is spread over two floors. The upper level of the main sanctum is dedicated to Lord Shantinath, the 16th Jain Tirthankar.

Blackwater of Pali Bhutiwali Dam

The blackwaters of Pali Bhutivali Dam create a serene and mystical ambiance in Maharashtra, India. Encircled by lush greenery, the dam's dark-hued waters offer a tranquil retreat. The reflection of surrounding hills and vegetation on the lake's surface enhances its scenic allure.
